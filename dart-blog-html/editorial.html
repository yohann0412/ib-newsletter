Note by the Editorial Team

“The secret of change is to focus all of your energy, not on fighting the old, but on building the new.” Socrates The words spoken by the Greek philosopher and thinker Socrates could not be truer. His understanding of change inspired us to take up the challenge of stepping outside our comfort zones and challenging ourselves to reach heights we never even dreamed of. IB Bridge so far has truly been an enriching experience. At first none of us knew what to expect, however over the past three weeks we have been slowly transitioning from one curriculum to another, learning new skills through the different modules of the bridge program and interacting with new students and teachers. During this time, we have been adapting to a new way of thinking and have understood the expectations of the IB program. There is a lot left to take in, but we’re getting there. Despite an eventful first couple of weeks, we realized that we pretty much have a long two years ahead of us which will definitely be filled with lots of learning as well as situations where we make mistakes. Our hope is that we can take something valuable from the same. With that we present to you the first IB Bridge Newsletter from the first Newsletter editorial team of the batch of 2023, which we hope provides an insight into what has happened so far. We hope you enjoy reading this newsletter as much as we enjoyed making it!

Best Wishes,
The Editorial Team
