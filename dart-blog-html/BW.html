Big Write- Delving into the written word

The Big Write (BW) module essentially enabled us to transition smoothly from our previous styles of writing towards the technique required for the IBDP program. It involves thinking critically in ways we didn’t really need to before as ICSE students, as well as contextually analysing the smallest of things

This module simultaneously prepares us to be able to communicate as effectively as possible and promote high-level research as well. The main rationale behind it is to introduce central areas of the extended essays, academic honesty, effective research and citation techniques. We covered the 3 domains of a learner - Cognitive, affective and psychomotor. We went into comprehensive detail about how each of these domains is necessary for a successful learner, and how they all correlate to our success in the real world.

The module consists of 12 parts which will conclude with the students producing one extended piece of writing on the topic of their choice.
