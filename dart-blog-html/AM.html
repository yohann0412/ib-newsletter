Assessment Module- Learn for learning's sake

Assessment Module is a module designed to foster a new attitude towards learning; to learn for knowledge and growth rather than the attainment of marks. We learnt that assessments in the IB Diploma Programme follow a unique Philosophy and Approach. The objectives are determined for each necessary area and assessments are designed accordingly so as to enable our growth in terms of self-management, and to gain a life-long passion for learning.

We framed questions based on our pre-existing knowledge and utilized certain guiding terms to take the same question to a higher level of thinking so as to challenge ourselves and to extend our learning. We arrived at the conclusion that assessment is an integral part of learning which consistently serves as a catalyst for growth in our journey of learning. The variety of assessments in the IB program is indirectly designed by all stakeholders of the learning group, at different stages, which greatly strengthens the learning outcome.

Over the next few weeks, we intend to discuss different types of Assessments and the multiple ways the assessments can be done. We also seek to discuss the most effective ways a student's understanding of concepts can be assessed.
