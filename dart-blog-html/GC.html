Global Citizenship- Becoming people of the world

What does it mean to be a global citizen? That’s exactly what we learn in this module of the bridge program. Through this module of the bridge program, students are able to appreciate the different cultures our world has to offer and the challenges which are posed in our quest to be global citizens.

During these past few weeks since the bridge program started, students learnt, understood and spoke about the obstacles posed by the world in order to be global citizens and learnt about how the media can play an active role in the same. Being a global citizen is becoming extremely important in today’s day and age looking at current world issues.

This module helps incorporate within us the values required for an individual to be a global citizen, such as: acceptance, empathy and tolerance; values which will definitely help us in the long run.
